Alleppey

I like Alleppey because it provides a serene experience. It is known for its backwater and houseboat rides. The traditional wooden houseboats offers a unique and peaceful way to explore the backwaters.

Three activities to do in Alleppey

  1. Houseboat rides
  2. food
  3. Monuments

Famous dishes at Alleppey

  • Fish curry
  • Appam
  • Puttu
